Interaction flow



Interaction Flow:

  1. Enter's password to phone and launches Chrome browser.
  2. Enter's www.wnyc.org in to the browser.
  3. Upon viewing the front page LLL scrolls through the news topics for anything of immediate interest.
  4. LLL then turns his attention to the center link on the header the reads, "All Streams" and taps.
  5. This brings him to a list of three live broadcasting stations, FM live cast, AM live cast, and FM2 live cast.
  6. Each live cast link shows what news program is being broadcast.
  7. LLL clicks on the station that seems the most appealing at the time.
  8. LLL lets the listening commence.
